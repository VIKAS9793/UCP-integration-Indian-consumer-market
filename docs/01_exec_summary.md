## What is UCP?

Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) is Google's open standard launched in January 2026 that enables AI agents to manage the complete shopping journey‚Äîfrom product discovery to purchase and post-sale support‚Äîacross any platform or modality.

**Core Value Proposition:** Eliminate checkout fragmentation so users can complete purchases wherever they discover products (AI assistants, search, social) without re-entering payment/shipping details.

## Key Risks

1. **Trust Barrier:** Indian consumers hesitant about AI making purchase decisions
2. **COD Incompatibility:** UCP designed for prepaid; 60-65% orders are COD
3. **Platform Resistance:** Amazon/Flipkart control 60%+ market, no incentive to adopt
4. **Regulatory Uncertainty:** RBI guidelines on AI-initiated payments unclear

---

## Entry Wedge Recommendation

**Start with D2C beauty/personal care brands** (Mamaearth, Sugar, Minimalist)
- High UPI adoption in customer base
- Low COD dependency (~15-20% vs 60-65% category average)
- Existing Shopify/Razorpay stack for faster integration
