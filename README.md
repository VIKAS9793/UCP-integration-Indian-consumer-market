**Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) for the Indian Consumer Market**

An independent product management case study analyzing Google's Universal Commerce Protocol and its fit for India's ₹10+ lakh crore e-commerce ecosystem.

---

## What You'll Learn

- **What is UCP?** — Google's open AI commerce protocol enabling seamless checkout across platforms
- **India Market Fit** — Why UPI dominance helps, but COD culture creates friction
- **Who Adopts First?** — D2C brands as the entry wedge (₹7+ lakh crore market by 2030)
- **GTM Strategy** — Stakeholder incentives and partnership requirements
- **Success Metrics** — North Star metric, KPI tree, and experiment design
- **Risk Analysis** — Trust blockers, failure modes, and governance controls
